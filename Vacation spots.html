Top Vacation Spots in Nigeria

By: Auntydogo
Published: 2 Days Ago

Nigeria is such a melting pot of cultures, there is never enough places to explore. Planning the perfect vacation involves packing up, leaving home hwerever that may be and experiencing something new.

1. Obudu mountain resort, Obudu

infamously known as Africa's biggest and finest tourist destination, its no suprise that this is the first destination on our list.If you are a tourist looking for a mix of adventure and idyllic tranquillity, then Obudu Cattle Ranch is the place to be. With rich natural vegetation and a picturesque view, Obudu is increasingly becoming popular amongst tourists from all around Nigeria and Africa as a whole.The ranch has numerous pleasant and beautiful mountain-area and country-side views, with a well preserved and calm Serene to suit all our guests.Learn More.
Top Attractions
  • Tour activities that include Holy Mountain,Canopy Walkway,Cameroon Border View,Igaga Waterfall,Angel Valley View e.t.c
  • Historical Monuments
  • Horseback riding

2. Lekki conservation centre, Lagos

Lekki Conservation Centre is a hidden treasure awaiting the exploration of tourists.It is one of the most visited tourist attractions in Lagos and Nigeria at large.The sole aim for the establishment of this conservation centre is to set aside a natural habitat for endangered plants and animals.So while on vacation,pack a pair of light clothing to be able to hike round the centre and enjoy the view. Learn More.
Top Attractions
  • The 2-km board offers excellent nature walks and bird watching opportunities
  • Family Park; A world-class picnic haven and offers nature walks, picnic & sporting installation
  • Nature Station
  • Koi & Tilapia ponds
  • 96-Seater Gazebos

3. Ezeagu Tourist Complex, Enugu State

The complex is located at the south central pan of the State.It has a unique beautification due to its natural setting. It is said exhibits one of the natural wonders of nature with a range of exotic foliage.It has also been said that the site of the water cascading down the rocks in the river is one of the most beautiful and relaxing things to experience.so if it sounds like an experience you are willing to go on, carry a pair of swimming outfit so you can go for a swim while you are at it.
